HTTP - HyperTextTranserProtocol

HTTP/1.1:
	Introduced during the year 1997
	Loads the requested contents one after the other
	its disadvantage is when any content has issues, the remaining content does not loads
	
HTTP/2	:
	Introduced during the year 2015
	Developers finds the drawbacks in HTTP/1.1 and found some solutions.
	Its Much Faster and More efficient than HTTP/1.1,
	Multiple contents loaded at a time, so if any issue in a content does not affects the other.
	Used Advanced compression method to compress messages to save some extra bytes
